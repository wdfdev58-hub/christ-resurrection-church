Welcome home

You were made
for more.

We are an ordinary people who have met an extraordinary God. Under the leadership of Pastor Odisitsi Victor Kooper, Christ Resurrection Church exists to see lives filled with hope — in Kuruman and across the community.

Sundays are warm and unhurried. You'll find honest worship, a message rooted in Scripture, and people who will know your name. Come as you are. Leave encouraged and sent.
